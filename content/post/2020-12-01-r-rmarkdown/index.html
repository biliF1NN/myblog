Data Storying In this project, it focuses on six different factors. The first factor is about weekend and weekday. It is clearly showing that weekday has the most car crash accidents than the weekend. To consider the second factor that is light condition and severity factor. It is clearly showing that serious injury accident and fatal accident are not happened during the dark light condition. Most of accidents are happened at day with good light condition. To consider the third factor that the speed zone illustrates that 60 KM/hr is the most dangerous speed zone. To consider the alcohol related accidents, most car crash accidents are happened at no alcohol time period. To consider the state division factor, most car crash accidents are happened at metro which is almost 3 times larger than country state division. The last factor focus on the different states to highlight the serious car crash accident across the entire Victoria state.
